How to Play?
  1. Press Play
  2. Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move the shell left or right
  3. Keep track of your lives with the heart symbol on the top right corner
  4. Beware of the spiked platforms and fragile platforms as they disappear
  5. Seek out purple stars as they enable you to move fast
  6. Stay away from bananas as they slow you down
  7. Play and make your way onto the LeaderBoard
